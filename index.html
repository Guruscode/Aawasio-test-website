OUR VISION

AAWACIO aims to provide assistance and support to the less privileged women, adolescents, and children. This effort includes ensuring that their health and well-being are enhanced.




MISSION STATEMENT

AWACIO believes that every human being deserves the right to have a good living condition and environment. We work to provide succor and stability to the less privileged women, adolescents, and children. Our actions are guided by integrity, compassion, respect, and accountability.

Strategic Goals

• Provide different kinds of relief to the less privileged.

• Provide humanitarian assistance to vulnerable children such as food, empowerment, and skills.

• Establish habitat projects for the less privileged to live in a good environment

• Ensure good water supply to the less privileged around the world.

• Provide boreholes to children and the vulnerable around communities and schools.

• Emphasize and encourage education for children where needed in the world.

• Provide means to empower women and widows to attain desirable financial status.
